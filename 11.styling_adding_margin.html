margin in Action

  • /* 20px of margin applied in all directions */
    margin: 20px;
  • /* 20px of margin applied vertically and 0px applied horizontally */
    margin: 20px 0;
  • /* 12px of margin applied to the top */
    margin-top: 12px;
  • /* 12px of margin applied to the right */
    margin-right: 12px;
  • /* 12px of margin applied to the bottom */
    margin-bottom: 12px;
  • /* 12px of margin applied to the left */
    margin-left: 12px; 
li {
    margin: 16px;
    }

Adding Margin

  • Margin - layer that provides spacing between the edge of the border and out toward other elements.
  • Clockwise direction - top, right, bottom, and left .
